About Us

Welcome to A1STEMAcademy! We specialize in 1-on-1 tutoring to help you excel in your studies. Our highly qualified tutors are dedicated to providing personalized instruction tailored to your learning needs. Whether you need help with Math, Physics, SAT/PSAT prep, Biology, or Chemistry, our goal is to help you achieve high scores and a deep understanding of the subject matter.

Math

Our Math curriculum covers a broad spectrum of mathematical disciplines, from basic algebra to advanced calculus. Each course is designed to build a strong foundation and prepare students for higher-level math challenges.

  • Algebra 1: Basic algebraic concepts and skills.
  • Geometry: Study of shapes, sizes, relative positions, and properties of space.
  • Algebra 2: Advanced algebraic concepts building on Algebra 1.
  • Pre Calculus: Preparation for calculus, including functions and analytical geometry.
  • AP Calculus AB: Introduction to limits, derivatives, and integrals.
  • AP Calculus BC: Comprehensive calculus course including sequences and series.
  • Integrated Math: Combination of algebra, geometry, statistics, and probability.

Physics

Our Physics courses explore the fundamental principles of the universe, from motion and energy to electromagnetism and thermodynamics. Each course includes both theoretical and practical components to enhance understanding.

  • Physics Honors: In-depth study of classical mechanics and wave phenomena.
  • AP Physics 1: Introduction to Newtonian mechanics, work, energy, and power.
  • AP Physics 2: Continuation of AP Physics 1 with electricity, magnetism, and modern physics.
  • AP Physics C: Intensive course with two sections:
    • Mechanics: Advanced study of motion, forces, and energy.
    • Electricity & Magnetism: In-depth exploration of electric and magnetic fields.

SAT/PSAT Math

Our SAT/PSAT Math prep courses are designed to equip students with the skills and strategies needed to excel in the mathematics sections of these standardized tests. We cover both the non-calculator and calculator sections thoroughly.

  • Non-Calculator Section: Focus on problem-solving and algebra without calculator assistance.
  • Calculator Section: Advanced problem-solving using a calculator, covering a wide range of math topics.
